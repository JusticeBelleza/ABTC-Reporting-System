🔐 Privacy Policy

ABTC ReportSys

Effective Date: 2026

ABTC ReportSys respects user privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information collected through the system. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

ABTC ReportSys does not collect patient-identifiable data.

The system only collects the following information for operational and reporting purposes:

📊 Aggregated Case Data

Number of animal bite cases

Statistical and summary data

Non-identifiable reporting figures

👤 User / Employee Information

Full name

Job designation or role

Email address

Contact number

No patient names, addresses, medical records, or personally identifiable health information are collected or stored.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

The collected information is used solely to:

Generate reports and summaries

Identify responsible personnel submitting reports

Support system administration and accountability

Improve system reliability and functionality

Data is not used for marketing, profiling, or commercial advertising.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

ABTC ReportSys:

❌ Does not sell data

❌ Does not share data with third parties

❌ Does not disclose data to external organizations

Data access is limited to authorized users only.

4. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect stored information from unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse.

However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill reporting and system purposes, unless required otherwise by applicable regulations or administrative needs.

6. Disclaimer

ABTC ReportSys is an independently developed system and is not affiliated with any government agency or private institution unless explicitly stated.
